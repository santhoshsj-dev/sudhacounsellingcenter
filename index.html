FAQ

How a psychologist can help me?

Most people manage to get through their problems by themselves or with the support of family, friends and loved ones. But sometimes, this support is not enough to overcome problems.

Psychologists are equipped with clinical skills and professional training to help people cope with mental health problems and life issues with counseling and Psychotherapy Techniques

+

Do only crazy people meet psychologists?

Psychologists are trained professionals who help people suffering from a variety of mental illnesses as well as help to manage meaningful and healthy life. Like:

A Psychologist can help you when you’re changing your life, help you with relationship problems, help you make healthy lifestyle choices etc.

+

When do I need a psychologist?

Here are five tell-tale signs that you need to seek a psychologist’s advice. If you experience any of these signs, make sure to seek professional help immediately.

  1. You are unable to function normally
  2. Your loved ones/family members are concerned
  3. You don’t enjoy things you normally do
  4. Your coping strategies aren’t working
  5. Your distress won’t go away
